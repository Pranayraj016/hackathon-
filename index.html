About

With a firm belief that only professional education has a potential to lay a strong foundation that can result in the blissful economic and social changes to the lives of millions, Rajiv Memorial Academic Welfare Society went about reshaping diligent and dedicated Indian youths in the form of global multifaceted talents through an array of Educational Institutions. Incepted in the year 1997, the group has demonstrated meteoric growth since the beginning. Today, it has become one of the most prestigious educational hub solely devoted to equip thousands of young students in the array of higher education in diversified fields i.e,. Engineering & Technology, Management, Pharmacy, Dentistry, Teacher Education, Basic Science & Library Science and Medical Science. Presently 12 institutes are thriving under the banner.

About the GL Bajaj Institute of Management

Welcome to GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida Driven by a strong inclination towards imparting quality education, GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the finest Educational Institute in the Greater Noida/Delhi-NCR region. Since the establishment of GL Bajaj Group, our skilled faculty share their knowledge with their students in the best possible way to find solutions to their complex issues and problems . Since its establishment in the year 1997, GL Bajaj Group has embraced quality and innovation favourable for the overall development of its students. Continuing on its path of growth, GL Bajaj Group experienced the greatest period of success in its history with 9 campuses located in NCR and Mathura. Gl Bajaj Group has been successfully building strong professionals in the field of Management, Technology, Education, Dental Science, Engineering, Pharmacy and Library Sciences. Thanks to Mr. Pankaj Agrawal, Vice Chairman, G L Bajaj Educational Institutions, whose selfless work has won laurels for the G L Bajaj Group at various platforms. G L Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the prestigious Institutions under the G L Bajaj Group offering Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA). The Institute is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CCS), Meerut.
